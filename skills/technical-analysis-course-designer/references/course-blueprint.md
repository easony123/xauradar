# Beginner Technical Analysis Course Blueprint

Use this reference when you need a ready-made lesson ladder, exercise ideas, or sequencing help.

## Core Path

1. Introduction to Markets and XAUUSD
Objective: Explain what XAUUSD is, what moves gold, and why volatility matters.

2. Candles and Price Bars
Objective: Explain open, high, low, close, bullish candles, bearish candles, and wick rejection.

3. Market Structure
Objective: Teach higher highs, higher lows, lower highs, lower lows, trend, and range.

4. Support and Resistance
Objective: Teach how to mark obvious reaction zones and avoid drawing too many levels.

5. Timeframes
Objective: Teach higher timeframe bias and lower timeframe execution without overload.

6. Indicators
Objective: Teach EMA, RSI, ATR, and ADX as supporting tools, not decision makers by themselves.

7. Entries, Stop Loss, and Targets
Objective: Teach setup planning, invalidation, and simple risk-reward thinking.

8. News and Event Risk
Objective: Teach why CPI, FOMC, NFP, and central bank messaging can distort clean-looking charts.

9. Trade Review and Journaling
Objective: Teach how to learn from wins, losses, and missed trades.

## Exercise Patterns

- Chart labeling: Ask the learner to label trend, range, breakout, or pullback.
- Level marking: Ask the learner to mark the nearest support and resistance.
- Setup filtering: Ask the learner to reject low-quality setups.
- Risk check: Ask the learner to choose which stop loss is structurally sensible.
- News filter: Ask the learner whether to trade before or after a major event.

## Useful Question Types

- Multiple choice for terminology and interpretation
- True or false for common misconceptions
- Short scenario questions for decision quality
- "What is wrong with this setup?" prompts for deeper understanding

## Good Beginner Mistakes to Address

- Mistaking every pullback for a reversal
- Treating one candle as full confirmation
- Using indicators without context
- Ignoring stop loss placement
- Trading into major news
- Overfitting lines and zones on the chart

## In-App Integration Ideas

- Show a "Learn this signal" card beside the live setup
- Add one short lesson plus one drill per screen
- Unlock later modules after quiz completion
- Reuse chart, history, and calendar pages as teaching surfaces
- Add a "Why this setup?" explainer tied to the current signal
